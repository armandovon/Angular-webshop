Order Confirmation

Once we have received your order, we will send you a confirmation email by email with information about your order. Save the order confirmation to be available for any contact with customer service. Always check the package contents and make sure it is consistent with your order confirmation.

Delivery

Usually you have your package within 1-3 days. For larger campaigns, it may take longer. Smaller orders with remaining item is sent when all items are in stock. We reserve the right to remove any remaining items from the order. When the shipment leaves our warehouse, a delivery message will be sent to the e-mail address you entered.

You choose the shipping method you wish at the checkout and you can choose the pick-up points that PostNord and Bring offer.

Larger shipment

Download from the post office. You will receive notification via SMS. Please note that packages over 20 kg are automatically shipped with Home Delivery (see below).

Home delivery

Scaling to the specified address, important that the port code is specified. The shirt bag can not be sent to box addresses.

Delivery takes place during the day between 08:00 and 17:00 on Fridays Monday to Friday. PostNord will contact you once via sms for delivery date and time window.

If you are not home on the agreed delivery date, you can download the package at the service point that PostNord announces. You can book a new drive via PostNord's customer service at 0771-33 33 10. New outreach will be done at an additional fee.
